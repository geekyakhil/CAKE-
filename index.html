My Chocolate Cake

Stuff You Need:

  • 1 cup flour
  • half cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cocoa
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • milk 1/2 cup
  • oil 1/4 cup
  • vanilla 1 tsp

How to Make It:

  1. Turn oven on to 180
  2. Put all dry things in bowl
  3. Add milk oil vanilla mix
  4. Put in pan
  5. Cook 30 mins
  6. Wait to cool
